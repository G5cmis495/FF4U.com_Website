Rules to creating your own team

Before you start creating your own team there are a few things you must know. A team comprises of 4 quarterbacks, 8 running backs, 8 wide receivers, 3 tight ends, 3 kickers, 3 defense players, and 3 special teams players, with a total of 32 players.
